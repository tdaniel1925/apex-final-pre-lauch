## DESIGN STYLE GUIDELINES

### ✅ DO:
- Use **clean, minimal layouts** (generous whitespace)
- Use **corporate blue tones** (navy, not bright blue)
- Use **professional sans-serif fonts** (Inter, Helvetica, Arial)
- Use **subtle shadows** and **borders**
- Use **high-quality icons** (outlined, not filled)
- Use **real photos** or **abstract concepts** (no stock photo clichés)
- Keep design **simple and scannable**
- Use **consistent spacing** throughout

### ❌ DON'T:
- No **rainbow gradients** or **neon colors**
- No **animated GIFs** or **flashy animations**
- No **countdown timers** or **fake scarcity**
- No **popup modals** on page load
- No **cheesy stock photos** (handshakes, pointing at laptop)
- No **Comic Sans** or **unprofessional fonts**
- No **cluttered layouts** or **cramped spacing**
- No **MLM clichés** (money raining, luxury cars, etc.)

## VISUAL REFERENCES

**Look Like These (Professional, Trustworthy):**
1. **Charles Schwab** (schwab.com) - Clean, corporate, minimal
2. **Fidelity** (fidelity.com) - Professional, organized, trust-building
3. **Lemonade** (lemonade.com) - Modern, simple, friendly (without being childish)
4. **Oscar Health** (hioscar.com) - Clear value prop, trust-building
5. **Stripe** (stripe.com) - Clean product cards, developer-focused

**Don't Look Like These (Flashy, Hype-y):**
1. Typical MLM recruiting sites (fake countdown timers, income claims)
2. "Get rich quick" websites (flashy graphics, testimonials with luxury cars)
3. Outdated corporate sites (Vanguard circa 2010)

### Desktop (1440px+):
