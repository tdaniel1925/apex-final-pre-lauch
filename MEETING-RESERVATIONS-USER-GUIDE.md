# Meeting Reservations - User Guide

**For Apex Distributors**

---

## 📋 Overview

The Meeting Reservations system lets you create custom event registration pages where prospects can sign up for your meetings, webinars, and events. Share a simple link, and prospects can register in seconds!

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Access Meetings Dashboard

1. Log in to your Apex dashboard
2. Click **Dashboard** → **Autopilot**
3. Click the **Meeting Reservations** tab

---

## ✨ Creating a Meeting

### Step 1: Click "Create Meeting"

Click the **"+ Create Meeting"** button in the top-right corner.

### Step 2: Fill in Basic Information

**Meeting Title** (required)
- Example: "Business Opportunity Webinar"
- Keep it clear and compelling

**Description** (optional)
- Explain what the meeting is about
- This appears on the registration page

**Custom Message** (optional)
- Special note shown on registration page
- Example: "Bring your questions!"

### Step 3: Set Date & Time

**Date** (required)
- Must be today or in the future

**Time** (required)
- Use your local time

**Timezone**
- Select your timezone
- Options: Eastern, Central, Mountain, Pacific

**Duration**
- Default: 60 minutes
- Range: 15 minutes to 8 hours

### Step 4: Choose Location Type

**Virtual Meeting** (Online only)
- Requires: Virtual meeting link (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
- Example: `https://zoom.us/j/1234567890`

**In-Person Meeting** (Physical location)
- Requires: Physical address
- Example: "123 Main St, City, State 12345"

**Hybrid** (Both virtual and in-person)
- Requires: Both virtual link AND physical address

### Step 5: Registration Settings

**Registration URL Slug** (required)
- Auto-generated from title
- Edit if needed
- Must be: lowercase, letters, numbers, hyphens only
- Example: `business-webinar-2026`

**Max Attendees** (optional)
- Leave empty for unlimited
- Set a number to limit capacity

**Registration Deadline** (optional)
- Prevent registrations after a specific date/time
- Leave empty for no deadline

### Step 6: Create Meeting

Click **"Create Meeting"** button.

Your meeting is now live!

## 📤 Sharing Your Registration Link

### Copy Link

1. Find your meeting in the list
2. Click **"Copy Link"** button
3. Paste anywhere (email, text, social media)

### View Registration Page

Click **"View Page"** to see what prospects will see.

### Your Registration URL Format

```
https://reachtheapex.net/YOUR-NAME/register/MEETING-SLUG
```

Example:
```
https://reachtheapex.net/john-smith/register/business-webinar
```

## 👥 Managing Registrations

### View Registrations

On each meeting card, click **"View Registrations"** to see who signed up.

### Registration Information

For each registrant, you'll see:
- **Name** and **Email**
- **Phone** (if provided)
- **Status**: Pending, Confirmed, Not Going, Needs Follow-up
- **Questions** (if they had any)

### Update Registration Status

1. Click on a registrant
2. Change status:
   - **Pending** - Just registered, not confirmed
   - **Confirmed** - Will attend
   - **Not Going** - Declined
   - **Needs Follow-up** - Flag for follow-up

3. Add rep notes (private notes only you see)

## 📊 Meeting Card Information

Each meeting shows:

### Stats
- **Total Registered** - All registrations
- **Confirmed** - Confirmed attendees
- **Needs Follow-up** - Flagged for follow-up
- **With Questions** - Registrants who asked questions

### Status Badge
- **Active** - Currently accepting registrations
- **Draft** - Not yet published
- **Completed** - Event finished
- **Canceled** - Event canceled

### Capacity Warnings
- "Only X spots remaining" - When close to capacity
- "At capacity" - When max attendees reached

## 📧 Email Notifications

### What Prospects Receive

When someone registers, they automatically get:
1. **Confirmation email** with:
   - Event details (date, time, location)
   - Calendar download link
   - Your contact information
   - Any questions they submitted

### What You Receive

You get notified when:
1. **New registration** - Instant email alert
   - Registrant name, email, phone
   - Any questions they asked
   - Link to view all registrations

## 🗑️ Deleting a Meeting

1. Find the meeting in your list
2. Click the trash icon (🗑️)
3. Confirm deletion

**Warning:** This will:
- Delete the meeting
- Delete ALL registrations
- Cannot be undone

## ✅ Best Practices

### Before Creating

1. **Have meeting link ready** (if virtual)
2. **Know your capacity limits**
3. **Set registration deadline** if needed

### After Creating

1. **Test the link** - Click "View Page" and complete a test registration
2. **Share early** - Give prospects time to register
3. **Check daily** - Monitor registrations and questions
4. **Follow up** - Contact registrants with questions promptly

### Sharing Tips

**Email:**
- Include link in email signature
- Send dedicated invitation email

**Social Media:**
- Post link on Facebook, LinkedIn
- Include compelling description

**Text Message:**
- Send directly to prospects
- Keep message short with link

### Setting Capacity

**Virtual meetings:** Can be unlimited or set high limit

**Physical meetings:** Set realistic limit based on venue

**Hybrid meetings:** Limit based on physical space

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "Registration URL already in use"

**Problem:** Another meeting uses the same slug

**Solution:** Edit the slug to make it unique
- Add date: `business-webinar-march`
- Add number: `business-webinar-2`

### "Registration Closed"

**Problem:** Meeting status changed or deadline passed

**Solution:**
- Check meeting status
- Update registration deadline
- Change status to "Active"

### "Event Full"

**Problem:** Max attendees reached

**Solution:**
- Increase max attendees limit
- Create second session

### Not Receiving Notifications

**Problem:** Email not arriving

**Solution:**
- Check spam folder
- Verify email in your profile
- Contact support
