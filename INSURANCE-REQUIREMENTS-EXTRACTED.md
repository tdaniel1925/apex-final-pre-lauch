## 2. Bonus Programs (from APEX Bonus Program.docx)

### Personal Consistent Production Bonus

**Eligibility**: All agents regardless of rank

**Threshold**: $2,000 commissionable target premium per week

**Bonus Structure**:
```
Week 1:         1%
Week 2:         2%
Week 3:         3%
Week 4+:        4%
```

**Continuation**: Must submit $2,000+ each week to maintain 4% rate
**Reset**: Failure to meet threshold resets bonus to 1% next qualifying week

**Quality Requirements**:
- 60% minimum placement ratio
- 80% minimum persistency ratio
- No chargebacks during qualifying period

**Payment**: Calculated monthly, disbursed by 15th of following month

---

### Quarterly MGA Base Shop Recruiting Bonus

**Eligibility**: MGAs only

**Base Shop Minimum**: $150,000 placed/paid commissionable target premium per quarter (90 days)

**Bonus Structure** (on total base shop premium):
```
9 recruits:     1%
12 recruits:    2%
15 recruits:    3%
18 recruits:    4%
```

**Recruit Definition**: Newly contracted agent who submits and places at least one policy during the same 90-day quarter

**Quality Requirements**:
- 60% minimum placement ratio
- 80% minimum persistency ratio
- No chargebacks during qualifying period

**Payment**: Calculated at end of quarter, disbursed by 15th of following month

## 3. Regional MGA Requirements (from Achieve Regional MGA Level.docx)

**Timeframe**: 18-24 months achievable

**Requirements**:
- Base shop production: **$600,000 annually**
- **6 MGAs across 3 generations**

**Override Structure**:
```
Base Shop (Gen 0):      20% on $600K = $120,000
1st Generation:         15% on $1.2M = $180,000  (2 MGAs × $600K each)
2nd Generation:          5% on $1.2M = $60,000   (2 MGAs × $600K each)
3rd Generation:          3% on $1.2M = $36,000   (2 MGAs × $600K each)
Bonus (4% on base):      4% on $600K = $24,000
```

**Total First-Year Compensation**: $420,000 (override only)

## 4. Beginner Agent Compensation Examples

### Full-Time Agent (from Beginner full time agent compensation.docx)

**Starting Commission**: 50%

**Weekly Production**: 2 cases/week
- Average case: $300/month premium = $3,600 commissionable
- Total: $7,200 commissionable premium/week

**Weekly Earnings**:
- Commission: $7,200 × 50% = $3,600 commissionable
- Advance (75%): $2,700/week upfront

**Bonus** (if write $2,500+/week):
- 4% bonus on total premium: $7,200 × 4% = $288

**Total Weekly**: $2,700 + $288 = $2,988/week

---

### Part-Time Agent (from Beginner part-time agent compensation.docx)

**Working**: 10 hours per week

**Starting Commission**: 50%

**Monthly Production**: 2 cases/month
- Average case: $150/month premium = $1,800 commissionable
- Total: $3,600 commissionable premium/month

**Monthly Earnings**:
- Commission: $3,600 × 50% = $1,800 commissionable
- Advance (75%): $1,350/month upfront

**Total Monthly**: $1,350
