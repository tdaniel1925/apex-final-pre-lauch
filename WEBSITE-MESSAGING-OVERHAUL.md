## ✅ UPDATED FAQ SECTION

**Q1: What exactly is Apex Affinity Group?**
> Apex Affinity Group is a technology company that provides AI-powered marketing and business intelligence software. We serve business owners across all industries — with specialized tools for insurance professionals. Our products are sold through a direct sales distribution model, meaning you can buy our software as a customer OR join as a distributor to earn commissions sharing it with others.

**Q2: Who are your products for?**
> Any business owner who needs marketing automation — insurance agents, financial advisors, real estate professionals, coaches, consultants, small business owners, and entrepreneurs. If you need a website, social media presence, lead generation, or content marketing, Apex replaces 5-10 disconnected tools with one AI-powered platform.

**Q3: What's the difference between member and retail pricing?**
> Member pricing is available to Apex distributors (people who join the business opportunity). Retail pricing is for end customers who just want to buy the software without joining the business opportunity. Example: PulseMarket is $59/month member, $79/month retail.

**Q4: Do I need to join as a distributor to buy the products?**
> No! You can purchase any Apex product as a retail customer without joining the business opportunity. However, joining as a distributor gives you access to member pricing (20-30% discount) plus the ability to earn commissions.

**Q5: What is the Business Center?**
> The Business Center ($39/month) is an optional back-office hub for Apex distributors. It includes your own branded website, AI-powered business tools, commission tracking, team dashboards, and discounted access to AgentPulse apps. It's designed for distributors who want to scale their Apex business faster.

**Q6: Do I need an insurance license to join Apex?**
> No! You can join Apex and sell our technology products (PulseMarket, PulseFlow, PulseDrive, PulseCommand, SmartLook XL) without any license. If you ARE a licensed insurance agent, or want to become one, Apex also offers top-tier insurance contracts and carrier appointments as a dual career path.

**Q7: What's the insurance component of Apex?**
> Apex offers a dual-ladder compensation plan. The Tech Ladder is for everyone (licensed or not) selling our software products. The Insurance Ladder is for licensed agents who also want to sell life insurance, annuities, and ancillary products through Apex's carrier partnerships. You can do one or both.

**Q8: How much does it cost to join as a distributor?**
> $0 to join. No monthly fees. No minimum production requirements. Many distributors start with just the software products they use personally, then build from there. The optional Business Center ($39/month) gives you advanced tools, but it's not required.

**Q9: How do I get paid?**
> You earn commissions on every personal sale (27.9% recurring monthly) plus override commissions on your team's sales (up to 5 levels deep). Commissions are paid monthly. Example: Sell one PulseCommand at retail = $139.37/month recurring income as long as that customer stays subscribed.

**Q10: What's included in white-glove onboarding (PulseCommand)?**
> PulseCommand includes a real team that sets up your entire marketing operation for you: social media accounts, YouTube channel, podcast distribution to Spotify/Apple/Amazon, custom domain configuration, content calendar creation, and AI voice cloning. Most clients are fully live within 10 days.

## 🎯 KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES

### DO:
✅ Lead with TECHNOLOGY and AI
✅ Explain what the products actually DO
✅ Show clear pricing (member vs. retail)
✅ Separate customer journey from distributor journey
✅ Use concrete examples and dollar amounts
✅ Emphasize "done for you" / "AI-powered" / "automated"
✅ Highlight insurance as OPTIONAL dual path

### DON'T:
❌ Lead with insurance or career opportunity
❌ Use vague terms like "services" or "solutions"
❌ Hide pricing or commission structure
❌ Conflate buying products with joining opportunity
❌ Use insurance jargon without explanation
❌ Make it look like an insurance agency
