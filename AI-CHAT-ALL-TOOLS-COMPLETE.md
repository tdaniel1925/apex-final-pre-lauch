### 📅 EVENT MANAGEMENT (3 Tools)

#### 1. **Create Meeting Registration**
**User says:** "Create a meeting for next Tuesday at 6pm"
**What it does:**
- Creates registration page with unique URL
- Handles virtual, physical, or hybrid meetings
- Validates all requirements
- Prevents duplicate slugs
- Rejects past dates

**Example:**
```
User: "Home Meeting, Tuesday March 25th at 6:30 PM, in person at 123 Main St Dallas TX, 60 minutes, max 20"

AI: ✅ Registration page created successfully!
🔗 Your page: https://reachtheapex.net/john-smith/register/home-meeting
📅 Home Meeting
🗓️ 2026-03-25 at 18:30 America/Chicago
⏱️ Duration: 60 minutes
📍 Location: 123 Main St Dallas TX
👥 Max attendees: 20
```
