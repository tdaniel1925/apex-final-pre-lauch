Our Story

Born from a love of
fire and flavour.

Chef Marcus Holloway spent a decade cooking in Lyon, Barcelona, and Tokyo before returning home with a singular vision: to create a restaurant that honours the land it sits on. Ember & Oak opened in 2018 with eight tables and a wood-fired oven imported from Tuscany.

Today, we work with over 30 local farms and producers, changing our menu weekly to reflect what's at its absolute best. Every dish is a conversation between fire, season, and craft.
