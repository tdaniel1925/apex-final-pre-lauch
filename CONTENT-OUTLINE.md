## ✍️ **TONE & VOICE GUIDELINES**

**Write Like:**
- A trusted friend who's found something real
- Honest, transparent, no hype
- Aspirational but grounded
- Conversational, not corporate

**Avoid:**
- "Make money fast!" energy
- Income claims or earnings examples
- Pressure tactics or false urgency
- MLM-style "opportunity" language
- Salesy, pushy tone

**Use:**
- "Build" instead of "earn" or "make money"
- "Help" instead of "sell" or "refer"
- "Recurring income" instead of "passive income"
- "Real value" instead of "amazing opportunity"
