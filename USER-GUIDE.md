## 💼 CRM System

### **Overview** (Business Center Only)

The CRM system helps you manage:
- **Leads:** Prospects you're working with
- **Contacts:** Converted customers
- **Activities:** Calls, emails, meetings
- **Tasks:** Follow-ups and to-dos

### **CRM Dashboard**

Access: Click "CRM Dashboard" in sidebar

**Stats Shown:**
- Total leads
- Active contacts
- Pending tasks
- Activities logged

**Quick Actions:**
- Add lead
- Add contact
- Create task
- Log activity

### **Managing Leads**

**1. Add a Lead**
- Click "Add Lead" button
- Fill out lead information:
  - Name (required)
  - Email/phone
  - Company
  - Source (how you found them)
  - Interest level (high/medium/low)
  - Tags for organization

**2. Track Lead Status**
- **New:** Just added
- **Contacted:** First touch made
- **Qualified:** Good fit for your business
- **Unqualified:** Not a good fit
- **Converted:** Became a customer/distributor
- **Lost:** Decided not to proceed

**3. Convert Lead to Contact**
- Open lead detail page
- Click "Convert to Contact"
- Lead becomes a contact
- Activities and tasks transfer automatically

### **Managing Contacts**

**1. Add a Contact**
- Click "Add Contact" button
- Fill out contact information
- Set contact type:
  - **Customer:** Buying products
  - **Prospect:** Potential distributor
  - **Partner:** Business partner
  - **Vendor:** Service provider

**2. Track Contact Status**
- **Active:** Engaged
- **Inactive:** Not currently engaged
- **Archived:** Historical record

**3. Track Lifetime Value**
- Automatically calculated
- Shows total revenue from contact
- Updated with each purchase

### **Logging Activities**

**Activity Types:**
- **Call:** Phone conversation
- **Email:** Email sent/received
- **Meeting:** In-person or virtual meeting
- **Note:** General note or observation
- **Task Completed:** Completed a task

**How to Log:**
1. Click "Activities" in sidebar
2. Click "Log Activity"
3. Select type
4. Add details (subject, description, duration)
5. Link to lead or contact
6. Save

**Activity Outcomes:**
- **Successful:** Good outcome
- **No Answer:** Didn't reach them
- **Voicemail:** Left message
- **Scheduled Follow-up:** Next step planned
- **Not Interested:** Declined

### **Managing Tasks**

**1. Create a Task**
- Click "Tasks" in sidebar
- Click "Create Task"
- Fill out:
  - Title (what needs to be done)
  - Description (details)
  - Priority (low/medium/high/urgent)
  - Due date
  - Link to lead/contact (optional)

**2. Task Status**
- **Pending:** Not started
- **In Progress:** Working on it
- **Completed:** Done
- **Cancelled:** No longer needed

**3. Quick Complete**
- Click checkmark icon on task
- Automatically marks as completed
- Sets completion timestamp

**4. Overdue Tasks**
- Highlighted in red
- Shows in CRM dashboard
- Sorted by priority

## ❓ FAQ

### **General Questions**

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your email, check your inbox for reset link.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, go to Profile > Settings > Update Email.

**Q: How do I view my commissions?**
A: Click "Commissions" in sidebar to see your commission history and breakdowns.

### **AI Features**

**Q: What happens when I hit my free tier AI limit?**
A: You'll see a modal explaining you've hit your limit (20 messages/day or 50 voice minutes/month) with an option to upgrade to Business Center for unlimited access.

**Q: How accurate are AI Team Insights?**
A: AI analyzes real data from your team (QV, ranks, activity) but recommendations are suggestions, not guarantees. Use your judgment.

**Q: When are AI insights generated?**
A: Every morning at 6:00 AM CST, AI analyzes your team and generates 2-3 recommendations.

### **CRM Questions**

**Q: Can I import leads from a spreadsheet?**
A: Not currently available. Add leads individually or use the API.

**Q: What's the difference between a lead and a contact?**
A: **Leads** are prospects you're working with. **Contacts** are converted leads (customers/distributors). Convert a lead once they buy or join.

**Q: Can I delete a lead?**
A: Yes, but it's permanent. Consider marking as "Lost" instead to keep historical data.

**Q: Do activities transfer when converting a lead?**
A: Yes! When you convert a lead to a contact, all activities and pending tasks automatically transfer.

### **Organization Questions**

**Q: Why does my organization tree only show 5 levels?**
A: Performance optimization. For deeper analysis, upgrade to Business Center and use Interactive Genealogy.

**Q: What's the difference between Organization and Matrix?**
A: **Organization** shows your enrollment tree (people YOU recruited). **Matrix** shows the forced 5×7 matrix including spillover from your upline.

**Q: Can I see inactive team members?**
A: Yes, they're included in the tree. Business Center users get AI alerts about inactive members.

### **Business Center Questions**

**Q: Can I cancel Business Center anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: What happens to my CRM data if I cancel?**
A: Data is preserved but you can't access CRM features. Re-subscribe to regain access.

**Q: Is there a family/team discount?**
A: Contact support for team subscription options.
