INDEPENDENT REPRESENTATIVE AGREEMENT

This Independent Representative Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date of signature below, between Ascend Financial Services, Inc. ("Company"), a Delaware corporation, and the undersigned individual ("Representative").

1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

Representative acknowledges and agrees that they are an independent contractor and not an employee of the Company. Representative shall have no authority to bind the Company to any contract or obligation without prior written consent. Representative is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes on compensation received under this Agreement.

2. COMPENSATION

Representative shall be entitled to commissions as set forth in the Company's current Compensation Plan, which may be amended from time to time at the Company's sole discretion. Commissions are earned upon the completion of qualifying sales and are subject to applicable chargebacks and adjustments as described in the Compensation Plan.

3. COMPLIANCE AND LICENSING

Representative agrees to maintain all required licenses and registrations necessary to conduct business in their jurisdiction. Representative shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and Company policies, including but not limited to FINRA regulations, state insurance laws, and the Company's Code of Ethics.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY

Representative agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information, trade secrets, customer data, and business strategies of the Company. This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

5. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Representative agrees not to solicit any customers or representatives of the Company for the benefit of any competing business.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall commence on the date of signature and continue until terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days written notice. The Company reserves the right to terminate this Agreement immediately for cause, including but not limited to violation of Company policies, regulatory violations, or fraudulent activity.
