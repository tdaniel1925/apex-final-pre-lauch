APEX NETWORK INDEPENDENT REPRESENTATIVE AGREEMENT

Effective Date: November 14, 2024  ·  Document ID: APX-AGR-2024-58291

This Independent Representative Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Apex Network, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and the individual or entity identified in the enrollment form ("Representative").

1. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS. Representative is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of the Company. Representative is solely responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes, self-employment taxes, insurance, and all other business expenses incurred in connection with Representative's business activities. The Company will not withhold any taxes from Representative's compensation.

2. COMPENSATION PLAN. Representative will be compensated in accordance with the Apex Network Compensation Plan ("Plan"), which is incorporated herein by reference and made a part of this Agreement. The Company reserves the right to modify the Compensation Plan with 30 days written notice to active Representatives. Continued participation after notice constitutes acceptance of the modified Plan.

3. PRODUCT SALES REQUIREMENT. To remain active and eligible for commissions and bonuses, Representative must maintain a minimum of 50 Personal Volume (PV) per calendar month through legitimate retail sales to end consumers who are not enrolled as Representatives. Failure to meet this requirement will result in forfeiture of commissions for that month.

4. INCOME REPRESENTATIONS. Representative agrees not to make income representations that are not consistent with the Company's official Income Disclosure Statement ("IDS"). Representative acknowledges having reviewed the IDS and understands that most Representatives earn little to no income, and that income results depend on individual effort, market conditions, and other factors outside the Company's control.

5. ETHICAL CONDUCT & COMPLIANCE. Representative agrees to conduct business in an ethical manner, comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations, and adhere to the Company's Policies and Procedures as amended from time to time. Representative shall not make false or misleading claims about the Company's products, services, or business opportunity.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY. Representative is granted a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Company's trademarks, trade names, and copyrighted materials solely for the purpose of promoting the Company's products and business opportunity in accordance with the Company's brand guidelines. This license terminates immediately upon termination of this Agreement.

7. TERM AND TERMINATION. This Agreement is effective upon acceptance and continues on an annual basis, renewing automatically unless terminated. Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. The Company may terminate immediately for cause, including but not limited to violation of the Policies and Procedures, fraudulent activity, or conduct detrimental to the Company or its Representatives.

8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. In no event shall the Company be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages arising out of or related to this Agreement or Representative's business activities, even if the Company has been advised of the possibility of such damages. The Company's total liability shall not exceed the total commissions paid to Representative in the 12 months preceding the claim.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association, conducted in Wilmington, Delaware. The arbitrator's decision shall be final and binding. Representative waives the right to participate in any class action lawsuit or class-wide arbitration against the Company.

10. GOVERNING LAW & ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware without regard to conflict of law principles. This Agreement, together with the Policies and Procedures and Compensation Plan, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, representations, and understandings. Any modification must be in writing and signed by both parties.

ACKNOWLEDGMENT. By signing below, Representative acknowledges having read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions of this Agreement, the Policies and Procedures, and the Compensation Plan. Representative further acknowledges reviewing the Income Disclosure Statement and understanding the nature of the business opportunity.
