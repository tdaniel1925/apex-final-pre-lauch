# 🎯 Apex Compensation Plan - Complete Explanation

## Quick Terminology Guide

| Term | What It Means | Example |
|------|---------------|---------|
| **PV** | Personal Volume - Dollar amount of YOUR sales | You sell $500 in products = 500 PV |
| **GV** | Group Volume - Dollar amount of TEAM sales | Your team sells $10,000 = 10,000 GV |
| **BV** | Business Volume - Commissionable purchase price | PulseMarket retail $79 = 79 BV |
| **Override Pool** | Money distributed to upline (5 levels) | $92.91 from a $499 sale |
| **Waterfall** | How revenue is split up | Price → BotMakers → Apex → You → Overrides |

**Simple Rule:** More PV = Higher rank = More override levels = More money

**Important:** BV is always based on the purchase price the customer pays. If they buy PulseMarket retail for $79, that's 79 BV. If they buy member price for $59, that's 59 BV.

## 1. HOW VOLUME WORKS (The Foundation)

### Every Product Has a BV Based on Purchase Price

| Product | Price (Member) | Price (Retail) | BV (Member) | BV (Retail) |
|---------|----------------|----------------|-------------|-------------|
| Business Center | $39 | N/A | 39 | N/A |
| PulseMarket | $59 | $79 | 59 | 79 |
| PulseFlow | $129 | $149 | 129 | 149 |
| PulseDrive | $349 | $399 | 349 | 399 |
| PulseCommand | $399 | $499 | 399 | 499 |

**How It Works:**
- Customer buys PulseMarket retail for $79
- System records **79 BV** for this sale
- This counts toward YOUR personal volume (PV)
- This also counts toward your team's group volume (GV)

### Two Types of Volume

**1. Personal Volume (PV) - Your Direct Sales**
```
You personally sell:
- 1 PulseMarket retail ($79) = 79 PV
- 1 PulseFlow retail ($149) = 149 PV
- 1 PulseDrive retail ($399) = 399 PV
────────────────────────────────────
TOTAL PERSONAL VOLUME = 627 PV
```

**2. Group Volume (GV) - Your Entire Team**
```
Your team sells:
- Person A: $300 in sales = 300 GV
- Person B: $450 in sales = 450 GV
- Person C: $600 in sales = 600 GV
- YOUR sales: $627 in sales = 627 PV
────────────────────────────────────
TOTAL GROUP VOLUME = 1,977 GV
```

**Key Point:** Your PV is ALWAYS included in your GV. GV = Everything you + your team sells.

## 11. VOLUME TERMINOLOGY SUMMARY

**PV (Personal Volume)**
- The dollar amount of products YOU personally sell
- Based on actual purchase price
- Example: You sell $500 in products = 500 PV

**GV (Group Volume)**
- The dollar amount your ENTIRE TEAM sells (including you)
- Your PV is always included in your GV
- Example: You sell $500, team sells $2,000 = 2,500 GV total

**BV (Business Volume)**
- Commissionable value of a purchase
- In Apex's plan, BV = actual purchase price
- Member price vs retail price = different BV
- Example: PulseMarket retail $79 = 79 BV, member $59 = 59 BV

**Why This Matters:**
- Rank requirements use PV and GV
- 50 PV minimum rule uses PV only
- Commissions are calculated from purchase price (BV)
- All three are dollar-based, not arbitrary points
