APEX Bonus Program:

Addendum: Bonus Program Provisions

This Addendum outlines two performance-based bonus programs: (1) the Personal Consistent Production Bonus and (2) the Quarterly MGA Base Shop Recruiting Bonus. These programs are available to qualifying Agents and Managing General Agents ("MGAs") as defined below and are subject to the terms set forth herein.



Section 1: Personal Consistent Production Bonus

1.1 Eligibility:All Agents, regardless of level or rank, are eligible to participate in this bonus program provided they meet the requirements specified below.

1.2 Qualification Criteria:An Agent must submit a minimum of $2,000 in commissionable target premium in a single calendar week in order to qualify. Upon meeting this threshold, the Agent becomes eligible for a bonus calculated as a percentage of that week’s total commissionable premium, according to the schedule below:

1.3 Continuation and Reset Policy:

To maintain the 4% bonus rate, the Agent must continue to submit a minimum of $2,000 in commissionable premium each week.

Failure to meet the $2,000 threshold in any given week will result in the bonus percentage resetting to 1% the next time the Agent qualifies.

1.4 Payment Terms:

Bonus amounts will be calculated at the end of each calendar month and will be disbursed by the 15th day of the following month.

1.5 Additional Conditions:To be eligible for payment under this bonus program, the Agent must:

Be in good standing (i.e., no chargebacks during the qualifying period),

Maintain a minimum placement ratio of 60%, and

Maintain a minimum persistency ratio of 80%.



Section 2: Quarterly MGA Base Shop Recruiting Bonus

2.1 Eligibility:This program is available exclusively to individuals holding the rank of Managing General Agent (“MGA”) who meet the production and recruiting requirements outlined herein.

2.2 Qualification Criteria:To qualify, an MGA must meet all of the following during a standard 90-day calendar quarter:

A minimum of $150,000 in placed and paid commissionable target premium from their entire base shop, including personal production and the production of all directly and indirectly recruited downline Agents and Managers;

A minimum placement ratio of 60%;

A minimum persistency ratio of 80%;

No chargebacks during the qualifying period;

A minimum number of qualified new recruits, as outlined in Section 2.3.

2.3 Recruiting and Bonus Structure:Qualified recruits must submit and place at least one policy during the applicable 90-day quarter. No minimum premium amount is required.

2.4 Definition of a Recruit:A "recruit" is defined as a newly contracted Agent who submits and places at least one policy during the same 90-day quarter in which they are contracted.

2.5 Payment Terms:

Bonus amounts will be calculated at the close of each calendar quarter and disbursed by the 15th day of the following month.



Effective Date:These bonus provisions shall become effective as of the date of execution of this Addendum and shall remain in effect unless amended in writing by both parties.
