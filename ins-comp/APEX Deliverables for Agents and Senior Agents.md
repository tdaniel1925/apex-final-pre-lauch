APEX Deliverables for Agents and Senior Agents



Agent Deliverables



1. Write Clean Business  

   - Objective: Ensure accuracy, compliance, and quality in all insurance applications and submissions.

   - Responsibilities:

     - Maintain high standards of accuracy and completeness in client documentation.

     - Adhere to APEX and carrier guidelines to prevent errors and minimize chargebacks.



2. Effectively Navigate APEX Systems  

   - Expectation: Gain proficiency in APEX’s tools, resources, and processes to maximize productivity and effectiveness.

   - Actions:

     - Complete onboarding and training on all APEX platforms.

     - Regularly review updates or enhancements to the APEX system to stay current.



3. Understand Role Responsibilities 

   - Objective: Clarify the division of responsibilities between the agent, up-line, and APEX corporate.

   - Key Actions:

     - Learn when to seek guidance from up-line mentors and when to contact APEX corporate directly.

     - Develop a clear understanding of the escalation process for different types of inquiries or issues.



4. Gain Insurance Industry Knowledge  

   - Focus: Build a foundation in insurance products, sales strategies, and business management.

   - Learning Areas:

     - Product knowledge and insurance fundamentals.

     - Effective sales concepts and client prospecting methods.

     - Business management skills for long-term growth.



5. Master Carrier Procedures  

   - Objective: Understand and comply with each carrier's unique policies and procedures.

   - Responsibilities:

     - Learn submission guidelines, underwriting processes, and claim handling protocols.

     - Build effective working relationships with carrier contacts to facilitate smooth transactions.



6. Embrace the APEX Culture 

   - Focus: Adopt and embody APEX’s values, standards, and mission in daily work.

   - Actions:

     - Participate in cultural orientation and ongoing development sessions.

     - Represent APEX professionally, upholding its reputation and values in all client and team interactions.

Senior Agent Deliverables 

(*Includes all Agent Deliverables plus the following responsibilities*)



1. Recruit New Agents  

   - Objective: Develop skills to identify, attract, and recruit high-quality agent candidates.

   - Actions:

     - Learn recruitment strategies and best practices.

     - Build a network and pipeline to sustain team growth and business expansion.



2. Instill APEX Culture in New Agents  

   - Focus: Act as a role model, fostering a positive, values-driven environment for new agents.

   - Responsibilities:

     - Teach and reinforce APEX’s standards and cultural values.

     - Mentor new agents in aligning their work with APEX’s mission and expectations.



3. Ensure Agents Meet Deliverables  

   - Objective: Support and hold agents accountable for meeting their goals and responsibilities.

   - Key Actions:

     - Regularly review agent performance, providing coaching and support as needed.

     - Address gaps or issues proactively to maintain high standards across the team.



4. Learn Leadership in Insurance Sales  

   - Goal: Develop the skills to manage and lead a sales team, preparing to become an MGA.

   - Focus Areas:

     - Training in leadership, performance management, and team development.

     - Experience with goal-setting, motivation, and organizational planning.



5. Master MGA Deliverables 

   - Objective: Attain the knowledge and skills required to fulfill all MGA responsibilities.

   - Focus:

     - Complete advanced training in MGA competencies.

     - Demonstrate capability in managing larger teams, business strategy, and operational oversight in preparation for MGA-level responsibilities.
