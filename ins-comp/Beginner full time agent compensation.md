Beginner full time agent compensation 

Compensation Expectations for Brand New Agents

Starting Commission:

50% starting rate

Average Case:

$300/month premium = $3,600 commissionable premium per case

Weekly Production:

2 case/week → $3,600 × 2 = $7,200 commissionable premium

Weekly Earnings:

$7,200 × 50% = $3,600 commissionable

Carrier advance (75%) = $2,700/week upfront

Bonus Eligibility:

Write $2,500+/week = 4% bonus on total premium

$5,400 × 4% = $216 bonus

Total Weekly Compensation:

$2,700 + $216 = $2,916/week
