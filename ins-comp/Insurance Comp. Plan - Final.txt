Downline Producer Definition: A downline producer is an active writing agent with a minimum of $2,500 per month in production. If the agent is not producing on an ongoing monthly basis, they will not be counted towards your downline agents. The agent must have produced a minimum of the last two consecutive months and have been with APEX for at least 90 days to be considered an active downline agent.
New Producer Definition: A new producer is defined as a producer who has been with APEX for 90 days or less and who has written their first piece of business.
Recruitment and Production Credit Policy:
If a New Hire, Pre-Associate, or Associate recruits an agent, they will receive production credit for up to 35% (of total combined production of anyone they have recruited) for the required amount needed to obtain their next promotion.
The remaining production requirement must be met through the agent’s personal production.
Until the recruiter achieves Agent status or a 70% contract level, the recruited agent will remain under the up-line for training and management.
Minimum Performance Standards:
All promotions require a minimum 60% placement and 80% persistency rate.
Agents who do not meet this requirement must improve their placement and persistency before qualifying for promotion.

Key Definitions
Downline Producer
An agent in your organization who has submitted a minimum of $2,500/month in personal production for 2 consecutive months within their first 90 days.
New Producer
Any agent who has been with Apex Affinity Group for 90 days or less from their contract date. Special onboarding support and training applies during this period.
Recruitment Credit
Up-line agents may receive up to 35% recruitment credit toward their promotion requirements when they bring on and develop qualifying producers.
Training Under Up-Line
New agents are trained and supervised by their sponsoring up-line until they reach the Agent rank (70% commission level). Mentorship is active, not passive.
Contract Rate
The percentage of carrier commission assigned to an agent based on their rank. Rates range from 50% (New Hire) to 90% (MGA). Paid directly by the carrier.
Override
Residual income earned on the production of agents in your downline organization across up to 6 generations. Overrides range from 0.5% to 15%.
All definitions are subject to carrier contract terms. See full Promotional Guidelines for complete eligibility criteria.
