## 💡 OVERRIDE QUALIFICATION RULE

### 50 Credits/Month Minimum

To earn ANY team override bonuses, you must generate at least **50 personal credits per month**.

**What This Means:**
- ✅ Sell at least 1 × PulseMarket member ($59) = 59 credits → QUALIFIED
- ✅ Sell at least 2 × Business Center ($39 × 2) = 78 credits → QUALIFIED
- ❌ Sell nothing = 0 credits → NOT QUALIFIED (no overrides)

- You just don't earn team override bonuses that month
- Re-qualify next month by hitting 50 credits again

## 🔄 COMPRESSION & ROLLUP

### What Happens If Someone Above You Isn't Qualified?

**Compression Rules:**
- If someone in your upline doesn't qualify (below 50 credits/month)
- Their override SKIPS to the next qualified person above them
- You never "lose" the override—it just rolls up

### Example:

```
Level 1: You (Diamond rank, qualified)
Level 2: Sarah (Gold rank, NOT qualified - only 30 credits)
Level 3: Mike (Silver rank, qualified)
Level 4: Customer makes $149 PulseFlow sale

Override Pool: $24.02

Mike earns L1 (30%): $7.21 (his personal enrollee)
Sarah earns: $0.00 (not qualified)
You earn L2 (25%): $6.01 (COMPRESSED from Sarah's position)
```

**Key Point:** Compression rewards ACTIVE distributors who stay qualified!

## 📞 COMMON QUESTIONS

### Q: Do I have to recruit to make money?

**A:** For Pulse products, NO! You can earn great money just from personal sales:
- 10 PulseMarket sales = $164.80/month
- 5 PulseFlow sales = $180.15/month
- 3 PulseDrive sales = $292.44/month

**For Business Center, YES!** Business Center pays $0 to the seller - you ONLY earn when your team subscribes.

However, team building MULTIPLIES your income through overrides on ALL products.

---

### Q: What if I don't hit 50 credits in a month?

**A:** You still earn YOUR direct sales commission (always paid). You just don't earn team override bonuses that month. Re-qualify next month by hitting 50 credits.

---

### Q: Can I lose my rank?

**A:** Yes, BUT you get a 30-day grace period. If you don't maintain the requirements for 30 days, you drop to the rank you DO qualify for. You can re-qualify any month with no waiting period.

**IMPORTANT:** You never lose your HIGHEST rank achieved (for rank bonus purposes). Rank bonuses are paid once per lifetime.

---

### Q: How do overrides work on my personal enrollees?

**A:** You ALWAYS earn the L1 override (30% of override pool) on anyone YOU personally enroll, regardless of where they end up in the matrix. This is the "Enroller Override Rule."

---

### Q: What's the fastest way to rank up?

**A:** Combination strategy:
1. Get EVERYONE on Business Center ($39) first
2. Upsell high performers to PulseDrive or PulseCommand
3. Teach your team to duplicate the same process
4. Focus on helping your team hit Bronze/Silver (counts toward YOUR rank)

---

### Q: Can I earn on all 7 levels?

**A:** Yes, but only if you're Diamond rank or higher. Most ranks unlock fewer levels:
- Starter: L1 only
- Bronze: L1-L2
- Silver: L1-L3
- Gold: L1-L4
- Platinum: L1-L5
- Ruby: L1-L6
- Diamond+: L1-L7

## 🎁 BONUS POOLS

### Bonus Pool (3.5% of all company volume)

**Eligibility:**
- Must be Platinum rank or higher
- Must maintain 50 credits/month minimum
- Distributed monthly based on rank

**How It Works:**
- Company takes 3.5% of all commissionable volume
- Splits among qualified Platinum+ leaders
- Your share = Your group credits ÷ Total qualified group credits

**Example:** If you're Platinum with 15,000 group credits and total qualified pool is 1,000,000 credits, you get 1.5% of the bonus pool.
