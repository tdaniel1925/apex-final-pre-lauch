## 📧 14-Day Email Series

### Week 1: Foundation & Study Setup

**Day 1: Why Get Licensed? The Income Opportunity**
- Licensed vs unlicensed income comparison
- Commission structure explained
- Impact on families (not just money)
- 90% pass rate with proper prep
- Action: Research state requirements

**Day 2: Choosing Your Pre-License Course**
- State approval requirements
- 3 recommended providers (ExamFX, Kaplan, America's Professor)
- What to look for (pass guarantee, practice exams, mobile friendly)
- Pricing: $100-$300
- Action: Enroll TODAY (no procrastination)

**Day 3: Your 2-Week Study Schedule**
- Week 1: Content mastery (videos, reading, first practice exam)
- Week 2: Test prep (3+ practice exams, weak areas)
- Study time: 2-3 hours daily (same time each day)
- Action: Block calendar for 14 days

**Day 4: Insurance Fundamentals You Must Know**
- 4 key principles: Insurable interest, indemnity, utmost good faith, law of large numbers
- 5 parties: Insured, owner, beneficiary, insurer, agent
- Contestability period (2 years)
- 40-50% of exam questions come from this
- Action: Memorize principles and parties

**Day 5: Types of Life Insurance Policies**
- Term vs Permanent
- Term types: Level, increasing, decreasing
- Permanent types: Whole life, universal life, variable life, VUL
- Key distinctions for exam
- Action: Create comparison chart

**Day 6: Policy Riders and Benefits**
- Common riders (waiver of premium, accelerated death benefit, etc.)
- When to use each rider
- Cost considerations
- Exam questions about riders
- Action: List all riders and uses

**Day 7: Week 1 Review + First Practice Exam**
- Review all concepts from Days 4-6
- Take first full practice exam
- Expect 60-70% score (normal)
- Review wrong answers carefully
- Action: Take practice exam, identify weak areas

### Week 2: Advanced Concepts & Test Prep

**Day 8: Underwriting and Risk Classification**
- How insurance companies assess risk
- Medical exams and health questions
- Standard, preferred, substandard ratings
- Declinations and postponements
- Action: Study underwriting process

**Day 9: Premium Calculations and Reserves**
- How premiums are calculated
- Mortality tables
- Policy reserves
- Cash value growth
- Action: Practice premium calculation problems

**Day 10: Policy Provisions and Exclusions**
- Grace period, reinstatement, incontestability
- Suicide clause (2 years)
- War, aviation, hazardous activity exclusions
- Misstatement of age/sex
- Action: Memorize all standard provisions

**Day 11: Beneficiary Designations and Settlement Options**
- Primary, contingent, tertiary beneficiaries
- Per stirpes vs per capita
- Settlement options (lump sum, installments, interest only, life income)
- Community property states
- Action: Practice beneficiary scenarios

**Day 12: Business Uses of Life Insurance**
- Key person insurance
- Buy-sell agreements
- Executive bonus plans
- Split-dollar arrangements
- Action: Know all business applications

**Day 13: Ethics and State Regulations**
- Licensing requirements
- Continuing education
- Advertising rules
- Replacement regulations
- Unfair trade practices
- Action: Review state-specific laws

**Day 14: Test-Taking Strategies + Exam Day Prep**
- Process of elimination
- Time management (2 minutes per question)
- Flag and come back approach
- What to bring to exam
- Night before routine
- Action: Take final practice exam, get good sleep
