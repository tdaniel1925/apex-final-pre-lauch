# Apex Back Office User Guide

## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your command center. Here's what you'll find:

### Quick Stats (Top of Page)
- **Personal BV**: Your personal Business Volume for the month
- **Team BV**: Total Business Volume from your entire team
- **Team Size**: Number of people in your organization
- **Current Rank**: Your current rank level
- **Monthly Commissions**: Your earnings for the current month

### Main Features

1. **AI Assistant**: Click the red banner at the top to chat with me! I can help you:
   - Create meeting registration pages
   - Check your team stats
   - View commissions
   - Send team announcements
   - And much more!

2. **Training Audio**: Listen to daily motivational and training audio

3. **Race to 100**: Track your progress toward the next milestone

4. **Autopilot Tab**: Manage your meetings and events

5. **Network Tab**: View your team structure and genealogy tree

6. **Commission Tab**: Detailed commission breakdown

## How to Create a Meeting Registration Page

**The Easy Way (Using AI):**
1. Click the red "AI Assistant Available" banner
2. Say "Create a meeting registration page"
3. I'll ask you questions about:
   - What the meeting is about
   - Who it's for
   - Key topics you'll cover
   - The tone you want (professional, friendly, casual, etc.)
4. I'll generate a compelling description for you to preview
5. Make any changes you want
6. Give me the meeting details (date, time, location)
7. Done! I'll create the page and give you the link to share

**What You Can Do With Meeting Pages:**
- Share the registration link with prospects
- Track who registers
- Edit the meeting details anytime
- Send email invitations to your team

## Understanding Your Ranks

Your rank determines your commission levels and bonuses:

- **Bronze** (Entry level)
- **Silver** (3,000 BV + requirements)
- **Gold** (5,000 BV + requirements)
- **Platinum** (10,000 BV + requirements)
- **Diamond** (20,000 BV + requirements)
- **Double Diamond** (40,000 BV + requirements)
- **Triple Diamond** (80,000 BV + requirements)
- **Presidential** (150,000 BV + requirements)

## Commissions Explained

### Personal Sales Override (L1)
- 30% commission on your personal sales
- Paid when YOU make a sale

### Matrix Overrides (L2-L5)
- Commissions from your downline based on your rank
- Higher ranks unlock more levels

### Rank Advancement Bonuses
- One-time bonuses when you hit new ranks
- Increasing amounts at higher ranks

## How to View Your Team

**Method 1: AI Assistant**
- Ask me: "Show me my team stats"
- Or: "Who joined recently?"
- Or: "Show me my genealogy tree"

**Method 2: Network Tab**
- Click "Network" in the top navigation
- View your team members
- See their ranks, BV, and status

## Sending Team Announcements

**Using AI (Recommended):**
1. Tell me: "Send a team announcement"
2. I'll ask what you want to say
3. I'll format it and send it via email to your team

**Important:** Announcements go to all active team members

## Getting Your Personal Links

Ask me: "What are my links?" and I'll show you:
- Your personal replicated website
- Your meeting registration pages
- Your referral links

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see my commissions"
- Commissions update daily
- Check the "Commission" tab for detailed breakdown
- Ask me: "What's my commission balance?"

### "My team count seems wrong"
- Team size updates in real-time
- Ask me: "Show me everyone who joined recently"

### "How do I invite someone?"
- Share your personal replicated site link
- Or create a meeting registration page
- Or use the VAPI voice agent

## Tips for Success

1. **Use the AI Assistant**: I can answer questions 24/7
2. **Check Your Dashboard Daily**: Stay on top of your progress
3. **Create Regular Meetings**: Use meeting pages to drive registrations
4. **Monitor Team Growth**: Ask me about recent joins and inactive members
5. **Track Rank Progress**: Ask me: "How close am I to my next rank?"

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I edit a meeting I created?**
A: Click the edit button (pencil icon) on the meeting card in the Autopilot tab

**Q: Can I see individual team member details?**
A: Yes! Ask me: "Tell me about [name]" or "Show me [name]'s stats"

**Q: How do I know if someone registered for my meeting?**
A: Meeting registration confirmations are sent to your email

**Q: Can the AI help me with strategy?**
A: Yes! Ask me about best practices, rank advancement strategies, and team building tips
