# Meeting Registration Pages - Complete Guide

## What Are Meeting Registration Pages?

Meeting registration pages are custom web pages where prospects can register for your events, presentations, or opportunity meetings. They're professionally designed and include:
- Event details (date, time, location)
- Custom message from you
- Registration form
- Automatic email confirmations

## Creating a Meeting Page (Step-by-Step)

### Using AI Assistant (Recommended)

**Step 1: Start the Conversation**
- Click the red AI Assistant banner
- Say: "Create a meeting registration page" or "I want to create a meeting"

**Step 2: Answer Questions**
I'll ask you:
1. "What is the meeting about?" (e.g., "Business overview presentation")
2. "Who is this meeting for?" (e.g., "New prospects", "My team members")
3. "What key topics will you cover?" (e.g., "Income potential, Product benefits")
4. "Any special details?" (e.g., "Refreshments provided", "Bring a friend")
5. "What tone?" (professional, friendly, casual, inspiring, or educational)

**Step 3: Review the Description**
- I'll generate a compelling 2-3 paragraph description
- You'll see a preview
- Say "looks good" to approve, or ask me to change specific parts

**Step 4: Provide Meeting Details**
- Date and time
- Location type: Virtual, Physical, or Hybrid
- Virtual link (if applicable) - your Zoom/Google Meet link
- Physical address (if applicable)
- Duration (optional)
- Max attendees (optional)

**Step 5: Get Your Link!**
- I'll create the page instantly
- You'll see an inline preview
- Copy the link to share

## Advanced Tips

### Creating Effective Descriptions

**DO:**
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Use conversational language
- Create excitement and curiosity
- Mention what makes this special
- Include a clear call-to-action

**DON'T:**
- Make it too long (2-3 paragraphs is perfect)
- Use hype or pressure tactics
- Make unrealistic promises
- Use all caps or excessive exclamation points

### Choosing the Right Tone

- **Professional**: For corporate prospects, formal business meetings
- **Friendly**: For most situations, warm and welcoming
- **Casual**: For informal gatherings, team events
- **Inspiring**: For motivational events, success stories
- **Educational**: For training sessions, product education

### Virtual vs Physical vs Hybrid

**Virtual** (Online Only)
- Zoom, Google Meet, or other video link
- Accessible from anywhere
- Great for wider reach
- Provide link in advance

**Physical** (In-Person Only)
- Home meetings, coffee shops, offices
- More personal connection
- Provide clear address and parking info

**Hybrid** (Both Options)
- Best of both worlds
- Provide both address and video link
- Specify which format is primary

## Troubleshooting

### "The preview isn't showing"
- Wait a few seconds for it to load
- Refresh the page
- Try opening the link directly

### "I made a mistake in the meeting details"
- No problem! Just edit the meeting
- Or ask the AI: "Edit my meeting about [topic]"
- Changes update instantly

### "How do I cancel a meeting?"
- Edit the meeting
- Change the title to include "CANCELED"
- Or create a new meeting for the rescheduled date

### "Registrations aren't coming through"
- Check your email spam folder
- Verify the meeting is published
- Ask the AI: "Is my meeting active?"
