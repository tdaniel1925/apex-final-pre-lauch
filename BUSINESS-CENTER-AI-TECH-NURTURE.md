### 1. Show YOUR Usage, Not Generic Claims

❌ **Bad:** "PulseMarket creates social media posts automatically"
✅ **Good:** "I use PulseMarket to create 20 Instagram posts in 10 minutes. Here, let me show you my account..."

### 2. Use Specific Technical Details

❌ **Bad:** "The AI is really smart"
