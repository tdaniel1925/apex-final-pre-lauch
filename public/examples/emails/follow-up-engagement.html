The 3-Part Solution (From Pages 14-22 of the Guide)

1. Switched From MQLs to PQLs (Product-Qualified Leads)

Instead of gating everything behind forms, they created a free forever plan with limited features. Users could start using the product in 60 seconds without talking to sales.

2. Implemented Behavioral Email Sequences

When a free user connected their first data source, they received a triggered email with a personalized video showing them how similar companies used the product. Conversion to paid: 18.3% (vs 2.1% before).

3. Built a "High-Intent Scoring System"

They tracked 12 behavioral signals (API calls made, team members invited, integrations connected, etc.). When a user hit 8/12 signals, SDRs got a Slack notification to reach out within 5 minutes. These leads converted at 41%.
