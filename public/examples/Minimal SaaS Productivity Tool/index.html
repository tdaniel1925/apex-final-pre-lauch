Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built for focus. Designed for clarity. Flowdesk removes the friction between planning and doing.

Smart Task Management

Create, assign, and track tasks with intelligent priority sorting. Subtasks, dependencies, and due dates — all in one view.

Timeline & Gantt View

Visualise your project schedule with drag-and-drop timelines. Spot bottlenecks before they become blockers.

Team Collaboration

Comments, mentions, and real-time updates keep everyone aligned. No more status meetings or lost Slack threads.

Analytics & Reporting

Velocity charts, burndown reports, and workload views. Understand your team's capacity and delivery patterns.

AI Automation

Auto-assign tasks, generate summaries, and surface blockers before they slow you down. Powered by GPT-4.
